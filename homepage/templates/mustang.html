Muktinath Tour Package 2024

3 Nights / 4 Days Overland Tour (7-8 People via Scorpio)

Tour Highlights

  • Travel with 7-8 people in a comfortable Scorpio
  • Visit famous attractions such as Muktinath Temple, Galeshwor Mahadev, Marpha Village
  • Sightseeing at Pokhara: Fewa Tal, Tal Barahi, Bindabasini Temple
  • Hot springs at Tatopani and scenic views of Jomsom, Rupsey Fall, and more

Outline Itinerary

  • Day 1: Kathmandu to Beni

    Morning: Veg-lunch at Kurintar

    Day Activities: Sightseeing at Kushma (Longest Bridge of Nepal) and Kalika Bhagwati Temple

    Night: Dinner + Stay at Hotel Beni (Hill View or Hotel Taj)

  • Day 2: Beni to Muktinath to Marpha

    Morning: Breakfast at Hotel + Veg-lunch on the way

    Day Activities: Galeshwor Mahadev, Tatopani Hotspring, Jomsom, Kagbeni, Muktinath Temple

    Night: Dinner + Stay at Hotel Marpha Inn

  • Day 3: Marpha to Pokhara

    Morning: Breakfast at Hotel + Veg-lunch on the way

    Day Activities: Visit Marpha Village, Rupsey Fall

    Night: Dinner + Stay at Hotel Prime or similar level

  • Day 4: Pokhara to Kathmandu

    Day Activities: Visit Tal Barahi, Fewa Tal, and Bindabasini Temple

    Morning: Breakfast at Hotel + Veg-lunch on the way

    Drop back to Kathmandu
