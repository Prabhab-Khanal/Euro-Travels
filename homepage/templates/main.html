Muktinath

A sacred pilgrimage site for both Hindus and Buddhists, Muktinath is renowned for its temple and the 108 water springs that symbolize purification.

Experience the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Kathmandu and Pokhara, where ancient temples meet tranquil lakes and majestic mountains.
