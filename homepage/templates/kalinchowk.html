Kalinchowk Temple Tour Package 2024

1 Night / 2 Days Tour (7-8 People via Scorpio)

Tour Highlights

  • Travel with 7-8 people in a comfortable Scorpio

  • Day 1: Kathmandu to Kuri Village

    Morning breakfast. Hike/Cable Car to Kalinchowk Bhagwati Temple and return to the hotel. Lunch on the way back. Drop-off at your nearby location (Kathmandu).
