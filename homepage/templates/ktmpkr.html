Kathmandu-Pokhara Tour Package 2024

6 Nights / 7 Days Tour

Tour Highlights

  • Explore the cultural heritage of Kathmandu and the natural beauty of Pokhara
  • Visit iconic sites like Pashupatinath, Swayambhunath, and Muktinath Temple
  • Experience scenic drives, sunrise views, and local attractions
  • Enjoy activities such as boating, hiking, and exploring local markets

Outline Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu

    Arrival at Kathmandu International Airport. Transfer to the hotel and relax. Explore the city at your leisure.

  • Day 2: Kathmandu Local Sightseeing

    Full day city tour including visits to Pashupatinath, Bouddhanath, and Swayambhunath. Evening free for personal exploration.

  • Day 3: Bhaktapur & Lalitpur Local Sightseeing

    Explore Bhaktapur and Lalitpur, including Bhaktapur Durbar Square and the Kumari Temple in Lalitpur.

  • Day 4: Scenic Drive from Kathmandu to Pokhara

    Drive to Pokhara via Manakamana Temple. Enjoy the cable car ride and check into your hotel in Pokhara.

  • Day 5: Pokhara Local Sightseeing

    Early morning sunrise at Sarangkot. Visit Talbarahi Temple, Bindhyabasini Temple, Gupteshwar Cave, and Devi's Falls. Evening free for leisure.

  • Day 6: Drive from Pokhara to Kathmandu

    Return to Kathmandu. Explore Newroad and Thamel for shopping and sightseeing. Evening at leisure.

  • Day 7: Kathmandu Departure

    Transfer to Kathmandu International Airport for your onward journey.
