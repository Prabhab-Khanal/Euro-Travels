Pathivara Temple Tour Package 2024

4 Nights / 5 Days Tour (7-8 People via Scorpio)

Tour Highlights

  • Travel with 7-8 people in a comfortable Scorpio
  • Visit Pathivara Temple, Kanyam, and Dharan
  • Explore scenic spots like Fikkal, Illam, and Chiya Bagan
  • Experience local culture and natural beauty

Outline Itinerary

  • Day 1: Kathmandu to Kanyam

    Travel from Kathmandu to Kanyam. Enjoy the journey and relax at your accommodation upon arrival.

  • Day 2: Kanyam to Thulo Phedi

    Travel from Kanyam to Thulo Phedi. Enjoy the natural beauty and prepare for the next day's hike to Pathivara Temple.

  • Day 3: Thulo Phedi to Pathivara Temple and back to Kanyam

    Hike from Thulo Phedi to Pathivara Temple (3-hour walk). Visit the temple and return to Kanyam for the night.

  • Day 4: Kanyam to Dharan

    Travel from Kanyam to Dharan. Explore the local attractions and enjoy the overnight stay in Dharan.

  • Day 5: Dharan to Kathmandu

    Drive back to Kathmandu. Complete your tour with a return journey to the city.
