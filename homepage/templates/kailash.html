Kailash Mansarovar Yatra 2024

11 Nights / 12 Days Overland Tour by Bus

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the sacred Mount Kailash and Lake Mansarovar
  • Complete the holy Kailash Parikrama
  • Experience breathtaking Himalayan landscapes
  • Explore Nepali and Tibetan culture

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Gorakhpur

    Pick up from station/airport and night drive to Kathmandu via Sunauli Border.

  • Day 2: Kathmandu (1400m)

    Arrival in Kathmandu, hotel check-in, evening Pashupatinath Aarati Darshan, and trip briefing.

  • Day 3: Drive to Timure (2000m)

    Visit Pashupatinath Temple, then drive to Syabrubesi/Timure near Nepal-China Border.

  • Day 4: Kyirong (2700m)

    Immigration formalities, drive to Kyirong for acclimatization.

  • Day 5: Drive to Saga (4500m)

    Scenic drive passing Ghungtang-La (5236m) with views of monasteries and Brahmaputra River.

  • Day 6: Lake Mansarovar (4500m)

    Drive to sacred Lake Mansarovar, complete Lake Parikrama by vehicle.

  • Day 7: Mansarovar to Darchen (4700m)

    Morning rituals at Lake Mansarovar, then drive to Darchen.

  • Day 8: Diraphuk (4900m)

    Drive to Yamdwar, start Kailash Parikrama trek to Diraphuk.

  • Day 9: Zuthulphuk (4790m)

    Trek over Dolma La pass (5620m) to Zuthulphuk.

  • Day 10: Drive to Saga

    Complete Parikrama, drive back to Saga.

  • Day 11: Drive to Kyirong

    Return journey to Kyirong/Timure Border.

  • Day 12: Drive to Sunauli

    Cross back into Nepal, drive to Sunauli border.
